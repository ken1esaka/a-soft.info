About "Purchase" and "Restoration" of the app to a previously purchased state.
1. Family sharing is not supported.
2. You can only restore apps downloaded with the Apple ID at the time of purchase.
3. "App Store" - "Account" - "Purchased" shows not only "Purchased" apps, but also "Downloaded but not purchased". You can't check whether the app has been purchased or not here.
4. Check your Purchase History (Apple's site)← Find the app and check if the amount of money is shown on the right side. If the amount of money is shown here, you can "Restore Purchases".
5. If the AppleID of the app you purchased is different from the AppleID of the app you downloaded when you purchased it, the message "There is no purchases to be restored." or "Can not restore with this Apple ID." will appear on the screen.
6. If you still can't get it after confirming the above, please take a screenshot of the screen with the app name and amount of money in "4. Check purchase history" and attach it to an email from "Contact Us" in the app, and we will respond first. (Please do not delete the original text.)
If the iPhone restoration process is successful, your data and billing information will be inherited even if you have changed models, but if you have to re-install the app due to a glitch, be sure to delete the app and re-download it with the original Apple ID and tap "Restore Purchases" on the App Store screen in the app.
If the message "Can not restore with this Apple ID." is displayed, please tap "Restore Purchases" on the App Store screen in the app. is displayed, it is important to delete the app once and try again.
However, please note that settings and memo data will disappear with the app if you have to delete it. For now, there is no mechanism to save data even if you delete the app.
