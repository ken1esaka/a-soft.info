For bug reports or inquiries, please use "Support → Report a Problem" within the app whenever possible.

Benefits of Contacting from Within the App

  • App name, version, and build number are automatically attached
  • iOS version and device model are automatically included
  • This helps us investigate issues more smoothly

Including a screenshot of the issue will also help us respond faster.

If the App Does Not Launch

If you are unable to contact us from within the app, please email us at:

staff@a-soft.info

When contacting us by email, please include the following information if possible:

  • App name
  • App version
  • iOS version
  • Device model
  • Detailed description of the issue

After reviewing your report, we usually respond within a few days. Please ensure that emails from a-soft.info can be received.

We are continuously improving the app to provide a better experience.
