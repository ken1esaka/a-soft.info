CMail

Photo Mail (Legacy Series)

Send photos instantly

Have you ever taken a portrait photo and pasted it into a blog, only to find that it appeared sideways?

CMail is a photo mail app designed to reduce the chance that photos appear rotated incorrectly on Windows or blogs.

  • You can insert the date and time at the bottom-right of the photo (can be turned off)
  • You can also add a short text note

It is convenient when you want to send a photo immediately by email.
If you register a destination email address in advance, sending photos becomes even easier.
(You can also enter the address manually each time.)

You can also choose whether photos taken in the app should be saved to the camera roll (the “Photos” app).

If you find any issues, please send a screenshot using the in-app contact feature.
