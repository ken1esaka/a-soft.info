Reverse

Find chords from piano keys

What is Reverse?

Reverse is a feature that lets you search for chord names from the piano keyboard.

By pressing the keys, the app detects the combination of notes and displays the matching chords.

Chord Search

To identify a chord, tap the piano keys to select notes.

Matching chords will be displayed based on the selected notes.

Keyboard Operation

Tap a key to select the note.

By selecting multiple notes, the app can determine the chord.

Example


C
E
G

→ C

Chord Candidates

Depending on the selected notes, multiple chord candidates may appear.

The same set of notes can sometimes be interpreted as different chords.

Check the Chord

Tap a chord candidate to display its position on the large keyboard at the top.

Tap the keyboard to hear the sound of the chord.

Sound

The speaker icon in the upper-right corner toggles sound on or off.

  • On → Sound plays when the keyboard is tapped
  • Off → No sound when the keyboard is tapped

Add to Memo

Chords found in Reverse can be added to Memo by tapping the Memo button.

Examples of Using Reverse

  • Identify chords you are playing
  • Check chords while composing
  • Learn the notes that form a chord
