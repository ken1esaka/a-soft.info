FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

No sound when playing a chord

Please check whether the speaker icon in the top right is turned OFF (mute).

  • Speaker ON → Sound plays
  • Speaker OFF → Muted

Also, if the iPhone itself is in silent mode, sound may not play.

Where should I tap to play the chord sound?

Chord sounds are played by tapping the keyboard.

The speaker icon switches sound ON/OFF (mute).

What is an on-bass chord?

An on-bass chord is a chord
with a specified bass note.

Examples 


C/E
C/G
Dm/C

On-bass chords are available only in the Pro version.

For on-bass chords, the large keyboard at the top
shows position markers including the bass note.

The bass note is also included in playback.

Only a few chords are displayed

In the free version, the number of chord types displayed is limited.

The Pro version significantly increases the available chord types.

Can I save chords to Memo?

Yes. Chords can be saved to Memo.

  • Add from Forward
  • Add from Reverse

Memo allows you to manage chord progressions.

Can Memo have multiple pages?

Memo page management is available only in the Pro version.

With the Pro version, you can organize chord progressions across multiple pages.

Number of chords that can be saved per page

In the Pro version, the number of chords per page is
virtually unlimited.

Can I share chord progressions?

Memo content can be sent by email.

The recipient simply taps the attachment,
which launches M-Piano and automatically imports the memo.

This makes it easy to share chord progressions with friends or band members.

Can I import chord charts?

Yes. The app includes a chord chart import feature.

Paste chord chart text,
and the chords will be automatically analyzed and imported into Memo.

Lyrics mixed in the text are not a problem.

If the import does not work

Possible reasons include:

  • Text that cannot be recognized as chords
  • Pro version feature limitations

Can I play chords by tapping them in preview?

Pro version only
In the preview screen, tapping a chord will play its sound.

Can I check chord progressions?

In Memo, you can tap chord progressions to review them.

You can also display chord buttons in full screen.

Can I change the screen layout?

In Setting,
you can swap the left and right panes.

What is the Pro version?

The Pro version provides the following features:

  • On-bass chord display
  • Expanded chord types
  • Memo page management
  • Chord chart import
  • Tap-to-play in preview
